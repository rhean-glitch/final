Our Chef's Biography

Chef Rhean Torregosa

Chef Rhean, a graduate of the Center for Asian Culinary Studies, honed her skills in renowned Manila restaurants before opening her own acclaimed Cebuano bistro. Her innovative fusion cuisine blends traditional Filipino flavors with modern techniques. She's known for her vibrant presentations and commitment to using locally sourced ingredients. Rhean has been featured in several Filipino food magazines and is a sought-after culinary instructor.

Chef Kimberly Viscara

Chef Kim culinary journey began in her family's restaurant in Cebu City, where she learned traditional Filipino cooking from a young age. She later pursued formal training at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris, returning to the Philippines to establish a fine-dining establishment showcasing refined Filipino dishes. Her meticulous attention to detail and elegant plating have earned him critical acclaim. She champions sustainable practices and collaborates with local farmers.

Chef Shymae Sumagang

Chef Shymae, a graduate of the University of the Philippines with a degree in Food Science, brings a scientific approach to her cooking. Her experience includes working in Michelin-starred restaurants in Europe before returning to her native Cebu to establish a unique culinary concept. She's celebrated for her molecular gastronomy techniques and creative use of Filipino ingredients. Shymae is passionate about food innovation and regularly participates in international culinary competitions.
